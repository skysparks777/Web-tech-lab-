Risen Devil

This artwork showcases a vibrant fantasy scene filled with rich, fiery elements. At the center is a character with angelic qualities, depicted with glowing butterfly wings detailed in orange and white swirls. A golden halo above their head further emphasizes their ethereal nature.

In the background, a shadowy dragon emerges amidst flames, adding a sense of power and mystery to the scene. The fire and glowing orbs scattered around create a dynamic atmosphere, highlighting the interplay between light and shadow.
